Our Story

Born from a real mom's real struggle

Frendi was created by a nurse and mom who experienced firsthand how difficult it can be to raise a picky eater. When her child wasn't getting enough fiber, it led to ongoing stomach and digestive struggles.

After searching for something simple, convenient, and kid-friendly, she realized there weren't premade fiber drinks made specifically for kids. Frustrated with messy powders and gummies that didn't work, she decided to create the solution herself.

"She created Frendi — a daily fiber drink designed to make life easier for parents and help kids get fiber in a way they can actually enjoy."

This is more than a product. It's the answer to a question thousands of parents have been asking for years.
